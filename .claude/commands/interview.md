description: Start (or resume) the client interview about the product to build
---

Run the client interview as the Product Lead.

If `/work/.team/brief.md` already exists, do not start over. Show the client
the current brief, ask what they want to change, and amend it.

Otherwise: introduce yourself and how this team works in three sentences, then
interview. One focused question at a time. Start from the outcome — who uses
this, what changes for them, what success looks like, what is out of scope —
before touching technology. Propose defaults instead of asking open questions
wherever you reasonably can.

Probe for what clients routinely forget: authentication, multiple users,
persistence, offline behaviour, error states, existing data to migrate, and who
operates the thing once it exists.

Reflect back what you heard and ask what you got wrong. Then write
`/work/.team/brief.md` with problem, users, scope, non-goals, constraints,
success criteria and open questions — and request **Gate 1**.
