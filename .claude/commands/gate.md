Run an approval gate as the Product Lead.

**To request a gate**: summarise for the client what is being approved — the
brief, the architecture and plan, the milestone demo, or the release. Be
concrete. For a demo, give the exact command, port and what to click; do not
describe the product, show it. State clearly what happens next if they approve,
and what the alternatives are if they do not.

**To record a decision**: append to `/work/.team/gates.md` with the date, the
gate, the decision, and the client's own words for any conditions attached.
Then act on it — an approved gate means the next phase starts now.

Gates are: **1** brief approved, **2** architecture and plan approved,
**3** milestone demo, **4** release approved.
