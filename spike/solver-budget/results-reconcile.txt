# HOST numbers (x86-64, HotSpot 21.0.11), single-threaded.

Hypothesis: the spike's floor of 8 is a property of its SCENE (a column
seeded ~57 units in the air), not of the solver. Lowering the drop height
should move the floor to ~4 and reproduce the backend engineer's result.

velocity it is the hard drop that sets it — not the depth of the pile.
